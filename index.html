Keep your Chrome extensions permissions under control.

Zen Permissions is an extension for your Chrome browser that gives you a better idea of what information you share with other installed extensions. On one screen. Nicely organized.

Go To Chrome Webstore!

Simplify permission management.

Control what personal data your extensions are accessing. With only one click!

VS.

Features

  • Auto detection of all installed extensions.
  • Status check and organization (enabled or disabled).
  • Permissions display.
  • Host permissions display.
  • Categorization of extensions into risk categories (high, medium, low) depending on their permissions.

About Zen Permissions

Zen Permissions is a an source Chrome extension developed under Apache licence. You can install it from Chrome Webstore here.
